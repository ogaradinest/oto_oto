Everyone wants to talk about the model. Almost nobody wants to talk about the unglamorous list of things that determine whether it actually works in production.

Before go-live

  • Data access tested with the same permissions the live system will have — not your admin account.
  • A fallback path for when the AI is wrong, confused, or down.
  • One person, by name, who is told "if this breaks, it's your problem first."

This list only matters if the strategy behind it was sound to begin with — read why most AI strategies fail before they start if you haven't nailed that down yet.
