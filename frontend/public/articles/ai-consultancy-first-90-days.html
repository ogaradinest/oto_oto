The first 90 days with a good AI consultancy should produce one working thing, not thirty pages about future possibilities.

What good looks like

  • Weeks 1-2: one process mapped end to end, with hours and costs attached to it.
  • Weeks 3-6: a working pilot on that one process, with real data.
  • Weeks 7-12: measured results, then a decision — scale it, fix it, or kill it.

This only works if the underlying strategy was grounded in reality — see why most AI strategies fail before they start. And the pilot needs the same rigour as a full rollout; nothing here skips the implementation checklist.
