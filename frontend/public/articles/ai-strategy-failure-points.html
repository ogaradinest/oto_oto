Most SMBs don't fail at AI because the technology doesn't work. They fail because the strategy was written for a slide, not for the team that has to live with it.

The three questions that actually matter

Before you write a single line about tools or vendors, answer these:

  • Which specific, repeatable task is costing us the most hours this quarter?
  • Who owns the outcome if the AI gets it wrong?
  • What does "good enough" look like, in writing, before we start?

If you can't answer all three, you don't have a strategy yet — you have a wish list. This is where most engagements should start, and it's exactly what we walk through in our first 90 days of AI consultancy guide.
